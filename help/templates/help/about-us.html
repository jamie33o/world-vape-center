About Us

Specialities

We are a Laois based Vape Shop, providing people with the opportunity to switch from smoking to E-Cigarette/Vaping. Our shop is located in Portlaoise town center. 18+ ONLY!

Some of the brands we sell include Voopoo, Liqua, Aspire, Nasty Juice, Puffin Rascal, Absolution, DRS, Hale, Vampire Vape, IVG, Vaporesso, Riot Squad, and Innokin, among others.

History

Established in 2017.

The business started in August 2017, and since then, we have stocked up on a huge variety of different e-juices from brands like Liqua, Aramax, Nasty Juice, Dinner Lady, YeTi, King's Dew, and many more. We also stock 50ml and 100ml bottles along with nicotine shots.

Starter and advance kits are available in brands such as Aspire, Smok, Innokin & Voopoo. Along with the kits, we sell coils/filters and various accessories.

We were introduced to E-Cigarettes back in 2015, and in 2017, we decided to open our own shop to help others quit smoking and gain more experience in this field.
