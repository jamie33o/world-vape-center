Payment Options

At World Vape Center, we take every precaution to ensure that the whole payment process is very secure. Your data and payment are protected by an SSL certificate. We currently accept payment from Visa Credit & Debit cards, Mastercard, Maestro, Discover and American express. We only accept Irish registered cards. We do not operate with your card data. The process is done through your bank and our payment gateway provider.

Please note that funds are usually transferred to our account immediately. However, all card purchases are subject to a 24-hour security check which can delay delivery times by one business day. Bank Transfer usually takes 3 working days.
